Ganga Aarti

The Ganga Aarti at the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi is a highly revered and spectacular daily ritual that takes place in the evening. This ritual involves the worship of the sacred river Ganges (Ganga) and is performed with great devotion and grandeur.

Spirtual Significance

  • Dashashwamedh Ghat

    Main

    Dashashwamedh Ghat is one of the most prominent and vibrant ghats along the banks of the Ganges River in Varanasi.

  • Sankat Mochan Hanuman

    The Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, the monkey god.

  • Manikarnika Ghat

    Manikarnika Ghat is one of the oldest and holiest cremation ghats in Varanasi.

  • Tulsi Manas Mandir

    The Shri Satya Narayan Tulsi Manas Mandir is a temple dedicated to Lord Satyanarayan, a form of Lord Vishnu.

  • Durga Temple

    The Durga Temple, also known as the Durga Kund Temple, is a vibrant temple dedicated to the goddess Durga.

  • Banaras Hindu University

    Banaras Hindu University is one of India's premier educational institutions located in Varanasi.
